 App that rank Angular repositories ( ?https://github.com/angular ) contributors and show them in one list. The app contain three distinct views: 
 
1. The list of contributors to all Angular repositories. The ranking is sortable by amount of contributions made by developer to all repositories, by amount of followers, public repos & gists he/she published. 

2. The details page of each contributor with all repositories he contributed to and his details. 

3. The repository details page where we can find other contributors.
